About Us

Toneology

Toneology is an application that is used to check skin color by submit a picture and then the toneology application will predict skin color and recommend a foundation that is suitable for your skin color. Toneology application will divide skin color into 5 categories namely, pale, light, medium, brown and dark. After your skin color is predicted, this application will recommend several foundation products that match your skin prediction.
